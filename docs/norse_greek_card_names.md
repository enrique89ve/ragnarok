## Naming Conventions

### Norse Mythology Sources
- **Surtr/Muspelheim** - Fire realm and fire giant references
- **Niflheim** - Ice/cold realm references
- **Yggdrasil** - World tree for earth/nature cards
- **Skoll/Hati** - Wolves that chase sun/moon for lightning/dark
- **Fenrir** - Great wolf for shadow/beast cards
- **Sleipnir** - Odin's eight-legged horse

### Greek Mythology Sources
- **Leviathan** - Sea monsters
- **Phoenix** - Fire rebirth
- **Hydra** - Multi-headed serpents

### Naming Patterns
- Fire: Ember, Flame, Cinder, Blaze, Magma, Pyre, Inferno, Muspel
- Water: Tide, Sea, Frost, Glacier, Maelstrom, Abyss, Niflheim
- Earth: Vine, Root, Moss, Thorn, Verdant, Yggdrasil
- Lightning: Spark, Zap, Bolt, Storm, Thunder, Tempest
- Dark: Shadow, Dusk, Night, Void, Umbra, Helheim
- Light: Glimmer, Dawn, Radiant, Sunbeam, Aurora, Daybreak
- Neutral: Stone, Rock, Iron, Granite, Mountain
